Top 3 Travel Destinations

Kyoto, Japan

Kyoto is known for its classical Buddhist temples, gardens, imperial palaces, and traditional wooden houses.

“A peaceful city full of heritage.” — Traveler A

Reykjavík, Iceland

Reykjavík offers dramatic landscapes, hot springs, and opportunities to see the Northern Lights.

“A land of fire and ice.” — Traveler B

Queenstown, New Zealand

Queenstown is an adventure sports hub surrounded by mountains and lakes.

“Adventure around every corner.” — Traveler C

Travel Tips

  • Pack layers for changing weather.
  • Always check visa requirements in advance.
  • Carry local currency for small vendors.
